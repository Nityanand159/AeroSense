Air Quality Health Guidelines

Protect yourself and your family by following these EPA-standard recommendations based on real-time AQI levels.

AQI Range Category Health Recommendations
0 - 50 Good Air quality is satisfactory. Enjoy outdoor activities!
51 - 100 Moderate Sensitive individuals should consider reducing heavy outdoor exertion.
101 - 150 Unhealthy (Sensitive) People with heart/lung disease, children, and older adults should limit outdoor time.
151 - 200 Unhealthy Everyone should limit prolonged outdoor exertion. Wear an N95 mask if necessary.
