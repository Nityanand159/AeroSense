About AeroSense

AeroSense was founded on a simple premise: Clean air is a human right. We leverage advanced IoT sensors and satellite data to provide real-time air quality intelligence. Whether you are a city planner, a researcher, or a parent checking the air for a morning jog, our platform delivers the precision you need to breathe easy.
